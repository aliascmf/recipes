Spinach, Tomato & Feta Tempeh

1. Chop the tempeh and tomatoes
2. Put a tablespoon of oil in a pan
3. Optionally add garlic, onion, and/or pine nuts
4. Squeeze the juice of one lemon into the pan
5. Add the tempeh to the pan and cook until brown
6. Add the tomatoes and spinach and wait for spinach to wilt
7. Crumble feta and add to taste
8. Serve
